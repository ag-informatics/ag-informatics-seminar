Workshop Meetings & Project Flow

  • Project Contract: meet with me within the first 3 weeks to discuss and finalize what you will do for your project. Paper or prototype?

  • Project Work-in-progress Update: present once to the class to solicit feedback and input on your project.

  • Peer Feedback: everyone will provide templated peer feedback for each update presentation.

  • Project Final Submission: [Research Paper] or [Software Prototype + Short Report]

Discussion Meetings & Topic Flow

  • Topic Analyses: I will assign materials (readings, videos, etc). You will create a Topic Analysis for each discussion meeting and come ready to voice your thoughts.

  • Moderator Topic Brief: Everyone will serve as discussion moderator once. You will lead the group in discussion and prepare the topic brief.
